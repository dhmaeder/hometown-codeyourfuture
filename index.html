Why Cape Town?

Cape Town, fondly known as the Mother City, is a place of incredible natural beauty, rich history, and vibrant culture. From its stunning beaches to iconic Table Mountain, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

Cool Facts About Cape Town

  • Home to Table Mountain, one of the New 7 Wonders of Nature.
  • One of the only places to see African penguins in the wild.
  • Boasts the oldest wine region in the Southern Hemisphere.
